Together everyone achieves more

eFarms (incorporated as FoodBank GrowMore Ltd, RC 1456012) is positioned to transform Sub-Saharan Africa into the hub that feeds the world by providing financial linkage between farm-investors and small holder farmers; and empowering passionate youths with the practical skills and knowledge in building sustainable agribusinesses.The happiness that resonates among all stakeholders when they pull together is because 'Together everyone achieves more'.
